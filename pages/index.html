About Me

Hello and welcome to my homepage. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences (NIEHS) part of the National Institutes of Health (NIH), USA. I completed my Ph.D. from the Department of Decision Sciences at Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. Prior to joining IIMB, I was working as an IT Consultant at Oracle India Pvt. Ltd.. I am working in application of spatio-temporal models to epidemiology and climate change under the supervision of Prof. Soudeep Deb from Indian Institute of Management Bangalore.

This year I am in the job hunt. I am looking for a postdoctoral position in applied statistics/Artificial Intelligence.


My primary field of work is related to bayesian statistics, time series data, spatial, spatio-temporal modeling, inference for random processes in epidemiology and climate change. I am also interested in sports analytics, especially problems related to soccer and basketball.

Previously, I studied at the Indian Institute of Technology Varanasi (BHU), where I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Besides statistics, I enjoy playing basketball, reading, watching sports and singing.
